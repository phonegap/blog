Config.xml <preference> changes – added KeyboardShrinksView and HideKeyboardFormAccessoryBar. UIWebViewBounce renamed to DisallowOverscroll. EnableLocation deprecated. HideKeyboardFormAccessoryBar will hide the form accessory bar (the Next, Prev and Done buttons) when the keyboard is shown for  handling input in a textbox.
